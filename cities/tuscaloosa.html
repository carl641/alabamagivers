Givers in Tuscaloosa

Mercedes-Benz U.S. International (MBUSI)

Automotive Manufacturing

$12M+ to local community since 1993; $250,000 to YMCA Child Development Room; $100,000 COVID-19 relief via CFWA; $700,000+ in donated vehicles (15 SUVs) to schools & colleges; 6-week annual Habitat builds; STEM education programs (Mechatronics, Youth Apprenticeship).

Tuscaloosa County (Vance) Nonprofits: Tuscaloosa Habitat for Humanity; YMCA of Tuscaloosa; Black Warrior Riverkeeper; United Way of West Alabama mbusi.com

Bryant Bank

Banking / Financial Services

$50,000 to West Alabama Food Bank expansion; Annual "Turning Banking into Thanking" initiative (since 2017) honoring 25 nonprofits; $200,000+ raised at Heart Walk events; Community Room provided free to nonprofits; Holiday food drives.

Tuscaloosa (Downtown & multiple locations) Nonprofits: American Cancer Society; American Heart Association; West Alabama Food Bank; Big Brothers Big Sisters; United Way of West Alabama bryantbank.com

The Westervelt Company

Land Management / Wood Products

Westervelt Foundation (established 2010); Major naming partnership with Saban Center's Earth and Sustainability Gallery; Charitable giving via Community Foundation of West Alabama; STEM and environmental education support; Nearly a century of community presence.

Tuscaloosa (Jack Warner Parkway) Nonprofits: Saban Center; Community Foundation of West Alabama; United Way of West Alabama westervelt.com

DCH Health System

Healthcare

DCH Foundation (40+ years of Foundation Ball fundraising; 20+ years of BBQ and Blue Jeans; Play Pink breast cancer fund); Free community health screenings; $17 in care for every $1 received from county sales tax; Healthcare scholarships; Nursing education partnerships.

Tuscaloosa (University Blvd) Nonprofits: March of Dimes; American Heart Association; American Cancer Society; United Way of West Alabama; Habitat for Humanity dchsystem.com

Tuscaloosa County Nonprofits: SmileTrain; Gleaners Food Bank; FIRST Robotics; Square One Education; disaster relief funds brose.com

Phifer Incorporated
