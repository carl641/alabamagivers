Givers in Mobile

Austal USA

Shipbuilding & Defense

$500,000+ annually to local community initiatives; Austal USA Charity Golf Invitational ($2.4M+ raised to date for United Way); $300,000+ raised for United Way through supplier network; $100,000+ in COVID-19 recovery grants; 3D-printed PPE for USA Health.

Mobile (Brookley Industrial Complex) Nonprofits: United Way of Southwest Alabama; Feeding the Gulf Coast; Boys & Girls Clubs of South Alabama; USA Health austalusa.com

Airbus Americas

Aerospace Manufacturing

Airbus Foundation corporate philanthropy; Flying Challenge (12-week STEM mentoring for students); Holiday gift drives for Salvation Army families; Aircraft parts donations to universities (A330 elevator worth ~$750K to USA); Employee volunteerism via +impact platform.

Mobile Aeroplex at Brookley Nonprofits: United Way of Southwest Alabama; The Salvation Army; Mobile Area Education Foundation; Exploreum Science Center airbus.com

Hancock Whitney Bank

$20,000 pledge to USA Social Justice Scholarship (dollar-for-dollar matching to $40K); Opportunity Grants (up to $25,000 to nonprofits annually); Employee United Way campaigns (~$250,000 annually); Financial Cents literacy program; Hancock Whitney Stadium naming sponsorship.

Mobile (multiple locations) Nonprofits: United Way of Southwest Alabama; University of South Alabama; 100 Black Men of Greater Mobile; Community Foundation of South Alabama hancockwhitney.com

AM/NS Calvert

Steel Manufacturing

Annual funding to partner nonprofits; Advisory board participation; Annual Family Day at Greater Gulf State Fair (4,000+ employees & families); Catalyst: Women in Industry Conference sponsorship; Environmental stewardship partnerships; Workforce development investments.

Calvert (Mobile County) Nonprofits: Mobile Baykeeper; Mobile Area Education Foundation; Gulf Coast Exploreum; United Way of Southwest Alabama arcelormittal.com

$190,000+ donated to Child Advocacy Center by owner Keith Palmer; Board membership on Child Advocacy Center; Major contributor to Ronald McDonald House Charities of Mobile; United Way Pacesetter-level donor; Youth-focused charitable programs.
