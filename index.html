About Alabama Givers

Alabama Givers is a comprehensive directory connecting communities with businesses and nonprofits that are making a difference across the state of Alabama.

From Birmingham to Mobile, Huntsville to Dothan, we catalog organizations committed to community service, charitable giving, and making Alabama a better place to live.

Whether you're looking to support local givers, find volunteer opportunities, or discover businesses that share your values, Alabama Givers is your go-to resource.
